Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GoLoans and how our service works.

50 Questions & Answers
Application & Status
01 What is the status of my application?

Your application has been received and is being processed. You should hear back within the day.

02 What do the loan request stages mean?

Log in to your account to check your request status. Here is what each stage means:

  • DocumentGoLoans has not received your verified bank statement. You need to access the Instant Bank Verification link.
  • ValidationYour application will be reviewed by an underwriter within the day. They may contact you if they need more information.
  • EvaluationYour loan request has been reviewed. GoLoans has contacted you for more information or documents — check your email or phone.
  • SignatureYou have been approved. Check your email for your loan agreement, review it, and sign it so funds can be sent.
  • DeniedGoLoans cannot grant you a loan today. You may reapply after 30 days.
  • CancelledRefer to the approvals, denials, and cancellations section for more details.
  • ExpiredYour loan request has expired. You may reapply at any time.
Applying / Starting a Loan
03 How do I apply for a loan?

Go to the GoLoans website and click "Claim your cash" at the top-right side of the page.

04 What type of loans do you offer?

GoLoans endorses short-term installment loans provided by its partner lender.

Eligibility & Requirements
05 What are your requirements?
  • 18 years old and above
  • Canadian citizen or permanent resident for more than 2 years
  • Residing in Alberta, British Columbia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Ontario, or Newfoundland and Labrador
  • Active Canadian bank account with more than 3 months of history
  • Stable job for more than 3 months
  • Monthly income above $1,800
  • Few or no NSFs/OPPs in the account
  • Low short-term debt ratio
  • No Koho bank account (incompatible with their payment system)
06 Can I apply if I just started a new job?

No. GoLoans requires employment for more than 3 months.

07 Do you accept child tax, ODSP, or government assistance?

Yes.

08 Do you provide loans for newcomers to Canada?

No. GoLoans only endorses Canadian citizens or permanent residents of more than 2 years.

09 Are there loans for people with past bankruptcy?

Yes, as long as you can provide your discharge document.

10 Can I get a loan if I have an existing loan with another lender?

Yes, as long as your debt ratio is within their threshold.

11 Which provinces do you serve?

Alberta, British Columbia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Ontario, and Newfoundland and Labrador.

12 Do I need to use a suretyship?

Suretyship is not mandatory. You may opt out, but additional evaluation and a credit check may be required. GoLoans says suretyship provides added protection if you default, and they offer cashback on the surety fee once the loan is fully paid and in good standing.

Documents, IBV & Profile
13 How do I upload documents or ID?

Log into your account, click your active loan request, and use the upload button. You may also use their secure upload link.

14 What are Flinks, Inverite, and other IBVs?

They are trusted Instant Bank Verification (IBV) providers. IBV lets GoLoans securely view a read-only copy of recent statements and helps verify identity, address, and other information.

15 I cannot access Flinks or Inverite. What should I do?

Try the other IBV provider. If both fail, send GoLoans a screenshot of the error message so they can contact the provider.

16 How do I change or update my profile details?

After submitting your application, you cannot change profile details yourself. You must email GoLoans support to request changes.

17 How do I reset my password?

Click "Login" on the GoLoans website, then click "Forgot password." You can also email support to request a password reset.

Decisions: Approvals, Denials & Cancellations
18 Why was my loan denied or cancelled?

The decision is based on specific criteria, not your overall financial worth. You may reapply in 30 days.

19 Why do you keep cancelling my application?

You must wait at least 30 days before reapplying if your previous loan request was denied or cancelled.

20 Why was I approved for less than I requested?

GoLoans lends responsibly based on demonstrated capacity. On-time payments may increase eligible amounts over time.

Renewal & Increases
21 Can I renew my loan?

Yes, provided you do not have cancelled or denied loan requests in the last 30 days.

22 Can I get a loan increase?

Yes, you may ask a customer specialist, but an increase is not guaranteed.

23 Can I repay early and renew?

Yes. GoLoans says repaying early can save up to 70% on the guarantor/surety fee.

24 Can I get another loan if I still have an active one?

Possibly, but GoLoans may require an advance payment to close out the active loan.

Rates, Fees & Terms
25 What is the interest rate?

GoLoans says its partner lender's loans have an annual percentage rate of 31%.

26 What are guarantor fees or surety?

Suretyship means GoLoans acts as a guarantor for your loan. If you cannot meet repayment obligations, the surety may step in and cover the obligation to the lender, subject to the agreement.

27 Is there a penalty for paying off early?

No. GoLoans rewards early repayment and borrowers can save up to 70% of guarantor/surety fees.

28 Do you offer flexible payment plans?

Yes. Customers can speak with customer specialists about payment plans.

Payments: Repay, Defer, Change Dates
29 How do I pay back the loan?

Repayments are auto-debited from your account according to the Pre-Authorized Debit Agreement. Advance payments may also be made by e-transfer.

30 How do I pay off early?

Contact the lender's loan servicing team to request your payoff balance.

31 Can I defer a payment?

To request a deferral, contact the lender's loan servicing team at least 48 hours before your repayment date.

Payment deferral is not available if:

  • It is the first repayment of your first loan
  • Your previous repayment was already deferred
  • Your previous repayment bounced
32 Can I extend my loan payment date?

You need to contact the lender's loan servicing team.

33 How can I dispute or change payment dates?

Contact the lender's loan servicing team at least 48 hours before the repayment date.

34 What should I do if I cannot make my next payment?

Contact the lender's loan servicing team at least 48 hours before the repayment date to explore options. Otherwise, you may incur an NSF fee.

35 What happens after a missed payment or NSF?

Contact GoLoans immediately after a missed payment. If you can send the missed payment by e-transfer, they may waive the error fee.

Funding & Disbursement
36 Why do they say "funds as fast as today"?

After you submit your application and complete IBV, GoLoans evaluates the request. If pre-approved, you complete your profile, receive the loan agreement, sign it, and funds are deposited through your chosen method. The process can take as little as 30 minutes depending on how fast you provide information.

37 How long do e-transfer and direct deposit take?

E-transfer can arrive within minutes after signing, though bank issues may cause delays.

Direct deposit may arrive the same day if signed before the daily cutoff, otherwise the next banking day. Bank processing times vary.

38 What if the loan amount was not credited?

If 24 hours have passed and you still have not received your loan, contact GoLoans support.

39 When will my loan be deposited?

Bank processing times vary. Refer to question 37 for typical timelines by method.

Balances & Payouts
40 Where can I locate my payout figure?

Log in to your dashboard to view your balance, or message GoLoans for the payout amount.

41 How much is my remaining balance?

Contact the lender's loan servicing team for your accurate remaining balance.

Operational Info
42 What is GoLoans?

GoLoans is a co-signor service that helps match borrowers with an available lender for their loan request.

43 What are the operating hours?

Online applications are accepted 24/7. Live support is available from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM Mountain Time, Monday to Sunday.

44 Where is the nearest store?

GoLoans has no physical branches and is 100% online. Its corporate office is in Edmonton, Alberta.

45 Will this affect my credit score?

GoLoans does not conduct credit checks. However, if you opt out of suretyship, a credit check may be required.

46 Is my information secure?

GoLoans protects personal data using encryption, secure IBV providers like Flinks and Inverite, and multiple security layers. They do not share personal details without consent and handle data under Canadian privacy laws.

47 How do I unsubscribe from marketing emails?

Click the unsubscribe button at the bottom of the marketing email.
