Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Early Repayment Program.

Can I still repay early if I have an active loan?

Yes. You can request an early payoff at any time during your active loan term. Contact GoLoans via email or SMS to request your discounted early payoff quote.

Is there a penalty for paying early?

No. There are no pre-termination fees or penalties for repaying your loan early. In fact, repaying early may save you money on your guarantor / surety fee.

How do I pay early?

Contact GoLoans via email at onyx-vip@goloans.ca or SMS at +1 (825) 254-6545 to request your early payoff quote. Once the discounted payoff amount is calculated, send your e-transfer payment to payments@goloans.ca.

Will paying early affect my ability to get another loan?

Repaying your loan early may make you eligible for a new loan sooner. Completing your loan in good standing — including early repayment — reflects positively on your repayment history.

I was offered a rebate, can I still claim that when I pay off my loan early?

Only one promotion can be applied at a time. If you choose to use the Early Repayment Program discount, you will not be able to claim a rebate under the Loan Cashback program for the same loan. Please review the Rebate program terms to determine which option is best for you.
