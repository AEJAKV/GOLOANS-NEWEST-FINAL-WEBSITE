There is no obligation to repay early. If you continue with your regular payment schedule, your loan will simply continue under the terms provided by your participating or GoLoans-approved lender.

  • Your original loan terms and payment schedule remain unchanged.
  • Early repayment is optional — there is no issue with continuing on your normal schedule.
  • You can revisit early repayment later, subject to your lender's terms and any available offer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Early Repayment Program.

Is there a penalty for paying early?

No. Early repayment may be available without a separate pre-termination fee or penalty, subject to your participating or GoLoans-approved lender's terms.

If your lender offers an early repayment savings option, paying early may help reduce eligible guarantor / surety fees depending on when you repay and the offer available to your account.

How do I repay early?

You can request an early payoff quote through the available early repayment request process. Your participating or GoLoans-approved lender will review your loan details, repayment timing, and applicable offer terms to confirm your discounted early payoff amount.

Once your quote is confirmed, you can complete payment using the instructions provided. Your loan will be considered settled only after payment is received and confirmed.

Will paying early affect my ability to get another loan?

Paying early may support future eligibility, but approval for any future loan or offer is not guaranteed.

Future eligibility depends on the participating or GoLoans-approved lender's review, approval criteria, repayment history, and applicable terms at the time of your next application or offer.

If I was offered a rebate, can I still claim it if I repay early?

It depends on the terms of the offer made available by your participating or GoLoans-approved lender.

In some cases, only one promotion, discount, rebate, or savings offer may apply to the same loan. If you use an early repayment discount, you may not be eligible to claim a separate rebate on that same loan unless the lender's offer terms allow it.

Review the rebate program terms or confirm with your GoLoans-approved lender before choosing which option to use.
