Who is Eligible

Responsible Repayment Reward
  • Your loan is paid in full
  • All payments were made on time
  • No payment errors
  • No deferrals, payment changes, or collections activity
Validated claims of rebates are given after loan completion. This rebate is a post-completion benefit and does not change your loan terms or payment schedule.
90 Days
to submit your rebate claim after loan completion — don't miss the window

Rebates are processed and issued on Wednesdays. Submit your claim before the processing day to avoid a one-week delay.

What the Rebate Applies to

Eligible for Rebate

  • Rebates apply only to what was offered to you. Either the surety/guarantor fee or interest
  • Principal, bank fees, and third-party charges are not eligible
  • Rebate amounts are confirmed after review

Not Eligible for Rebate

  • Principal loan amount
  • Bank or transfer fees
  • Third-party charges or service fees
Rebate amounts are confirmed after review. Only fees or charges explicitly offered and applicable to your loan are eligible.

Important Reminder

Don't Miss Your Window
Two things you need to know before claiming

90-Day Claim Deadline

Rebates must be claimed within 90 days from the date your loan is completed. If not claimed within this period, the rebate will be forfeited.

Wednesday Processing Schedule

Rebates are sent every Wednesday. Claims should be submitted before the processing day. Otherwise, the rebate may be sent the following Wednesday.
