How it Works

STEP 1

Complete Your Loan in Full

Finish the full repayment cycle successfully before submitting any rebate request.

STEP 2

Make All Payments on Time

Keep the account in good standing with no missed, delayed, or failed scheduled payments.

STEP 3

Request Your Rebate Within 90 Days

Submit your claim inside the 90-day eligibility window after the loan is fully completed.

STEP 4

We Review & Confirm Eligibility

Our team validates the account details and confirms whether the participating offer applies.

This rebate is a post-completion benefit and does not change your loan terms or payment schedule.

If approved, your rebate will be issued according to our processing timelines.

What the Rebate Applies to

Eligible for Rebate

  • Rebates apply only to what was offered to you. Either the surety/guarantor fee or interest
  • Principal, bank fees, and third-party charges are not eligible
  • Rebate amounts are confirmed after review

Not Eligible for Rebate

  • Principal loan amount
  • Bank or transfer fees
  • Third-party charges or service fees
Rebate amounts are confirmed after review. Only fees or charges explicitly offered and applicable to your loan are eligible.

Important Reminder

Don't Miss Your Claim Window
There are two important things to know before submitting your rebate claim.

90-Day Claim Deadline

Rebate claims must be submitted within 90 days from the date your loan is completed. Claims submitted after this period may no longer be eligible under the participating lender's rebate offer terms.

Lender Review & Payout Timing

Rebate payout timing depends on the participating GoLoans-approved lender's review, approval, and processing timelines. GoLoans is not the lender and does not issue rebates directly.
