Data Collection
Types of Personal Information Collected

GoLoans limits collection to what is necessary for the purposes identified in the Policy.

Identifiers and demographic information

  • First name, last name, gender, date of birth
  • Phone number, email address, username, password
  • Address, city, province/state, ZIP/postal code, country
  • Company name, profession, workplace
  • Social Insurance Number/SIN (where required)

Usage data

  • Cookies and other tracking technologies
  • IP addresses, device identifiers, browser and OS data
  • Time of requests, pages visited, referral sources, geolocation (where permitted)

Other information may be collected when you contact GoLoans by email or phone.

How We Collect Information
  • Directly from you when you provide information via forms or communications.
  • Automatically through cookies and similar technologies when you use the Application.
  • From third parties where you have given consent (for example, credit checks or credential verification).

Requested Personal Information is generally mandatory; failure to provide mandatory data may prevent us from providing the requested services.

Data Handling
Processing, Retention & International Transfer

Processing methods

We use physical, administrative and technical measures to protect Personal Information, chosen based on sensitivity and processing purpose.

International transfers

GoLoans is based in Canada and may transfer information to service providers located in other countries, including the United States and the Philippines. Those jurisdictions may have different data protection laws.

Retention

Personal Information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes set out in this Policy or to comply with legal obligations. When no longer required, data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Disclosure & Service Providers

We share Personal Information with affiliates and third-party service providers when necessary to provide the Service (hosting, payments, analytics, customer service, legal, accounting, marketing, etc.). Providers only receive the information necessary to perform their duties and are contractually required to protect it.

Your Rights
Purposes of Processing
  • Provide and improve the Service
  • Manage accounts and authenticate users
  • Communicate, send notifications, and respond to inquiries
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements
  • Analytics, marketing, remarketing and advertising
Cookies & Trackers

We use cookies and similar technologies for strictly necessary, functional, and analytical purposes. You can manage cookies via your browser settings or the cookie banner; note disabling some trackers may affect functionality.

User Rights
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Access the Personal Information we hold about you
  • Request corrections or deletions as required by law

Contact us at security@goloans.ca to exercise these rights.

Policy Details
Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Where required, we will notify you of material changes by posting a prominent notice or sending an email. Please review this page periodically.

Definitions
  • Personal Information: information that identifies or can identify a natural person.
  • User: the person using the Application.
  • Tracker: cookies and similar technologies.
