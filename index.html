Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GoLoans and how our service works

Is GoLoans a Lender?

No. GoLoans is not a lender. GoLoans is a loan matching service designed to connect applicants with vetted third-party lenders. We help evaluate, score, and route loan inquiries to financial service providers who review your application and reach out directly.

What Do Online Loans Really Mean?

Online loans are personal loans you can apply for entirely over the internet — no bank visits, no paperwork. The application, approval, and fund disbursement all happen digitally, making the process much faster and more convenient than traditional lending.

What is the easiest loan to get approved for?

An Installment Loan is the easiest solution to get approved for, as its application process is fast and straightforward. Primary qualification requirements include legal age and citizenship, a valid bank account, and a stable income source. Funds may be obtained as soon as the next business day.

How fast can I get my money?

Once matched and approved by a lender, funds can arrive in your account in as fast as 15 minutes. Using our Express Sign Up option speeds up the process by up to 70%, thanks to Instant Bank Verification (IBV) technology.
